Meet Our Founders

Dr. Elizabeth Moller

Dr. Moller was born and raised in Athens, Ohio, and graduated from Athens High School. She earned her undergraduate degree in Biochemistry from Virginia Tech and received a Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from The Ohio State University College of Dentistry. She attended the Louis Stokes VA Medical Center General Practice Program where she received her GPR certificate as well as extensive training in oral surgery and anesthesia. In 2000 she received her IV Conscious Sedation certificate from Miami Valley Hospital in Dayton, OH.

Dr. Moller has completed thousands of sedations and dental surgical procedures since receiving her sedation permit from the Ohio State Dental Board. She holds and actively maintains current certifications in basic life support as well as advanced cardiovascular life support. Dr. Moller practices as a general dentist and is proficient in all areas of dentistry. She has a special interest and is highly skilled in providing sedation and surgical procedures such as wisdom tooth extraction, bone grafting, tissue grafting, and implant dentistry. Notable advanced training includes affiliations with the Midwest Implant Institute, Pikos Surgical Institute and Kois Center for advanced dental education. She is a member of the American Dental Association, Ohio Dental Association, Northwest Ohio Dental Association, Academy of General Dentistry, American Academy of Implant Dentistry, and the American Dental Society of Anesthesiology.

Outside of work her hobbies include snowboarding, wakeboarding, and traveling. Dr. Moller also has many home improvement projects she is currently working on with her husband. Dr. Moller has completed nearly 500 hours of continuing education since graduating dental school and she is committed to mastering the most advanced techniques in order to provide the highest level of surgical and dental care for her patients.

Dr. John Enamel

Dr. Enamel was born and raised in Northeastern Ohio. He received his dental training at The Ohio State University College of Dentistry followed by a post-doctorate residency at the Miami Valley Hospital in Dayton. In this post-doctorate residency, he learned advanced skills and techniques in Cosmetic Dentistry, Crown and Bridge and Root Canal Therapy. In addition to exceptional abilities in general and cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Enamel brings an extensive set of IV conscious sedation and oral surgery skills.

Dr. Enamel has a passion for giving his patients the best dental care using the latest technology in dentistry. Always wanting to give back to his community, Dr. Enamel participates in the “OHIO Project”, a program that helps giving dental care to the less fortunate in underserved areas of Ohio.

He is a member of the American Dental Association, Ohio Dental Association, and Columbus Dental Society. Dr. Enamel regularly attends Continuing Education courses to keep abreast of the newest techniques in the dental field.

Dr. Enamel married his wife, Erin, in the spring of 2011. He is very active and enjoys the outdoors. He is an avid mountain biker, jogger, golfer, and he loves to fish. During the fall, you can find him watching and cheering on Buckeye football.

Dr. Susan Incisor

Originally from Dayton, Dr. Incisor attended The Ohio State University where she majored in Nutrition and continued on to obtain her Doctorate of Dental Surgery. Dr. Incisor's main focus is to provide the best dental care while making the patient feel entirely at ease. She believes it is important to treat each patient as if they are family. "Pearly Whites Family Dentistry is a place where you can have all of your dental needs met at the highest standard of care without the fear of being lectured."

Dr. Incisor is a member of the American Dental Association, the Ohio Dental Association, and the Dayton Dental Association. A firm believer that one's education should never end, Dr.Incisor participates in many continuing education courses.

While not in the office, Dr. Incisor enjoys spending time with her husband, son, and her two dogs. Her favorite activities include jogging, hiking, concerts, trying new restaurants, and cheering on Ohio State. Go Bucks!
